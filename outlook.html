Intuitive Outlook Calendar

Enhance feature for Outlook Calendar app for Outside Sellers

Role: UX/UI Designer Team: Individual Project: Design Assignment Device: Android Duration: 5 Days

Project Overview
Context

It was my design assignment project. In this project I was did ux research, personas, storyboarding to final design.

The Challenge

Design a flow demonstrating the experience of an outside seller getting ready for his upcoming working day using Outlook calendar, but not restricting to what it provides. The goal is to enrich the current experience and make it more tailored for outside sellers.

Goal

- To design a Calendar app well suited for Outside Sellers.
- To demonstrate the understanding of the Interaction & Visual Design
- To showcase the design process

Process
Research

A brief research was done to understand the outside sellers life in a day and to determine the problems they face. In the process I interviewed few sellers from Microsoft GD to gather the information around the tasks these sellers perform, their needs, expectations and what drives their motivation; I also analyzed the scope of calendar as a tool in a sellers life. In addition, I also assessed the competitors market positioning and analyzed their website functionalities/features; which really helped me identify the key points and gaps and what difficulties the users face when they use a calendar app. A small empathy research was also don over a call with couple of sellers.

Empathy Map
Personas
Synthesizing User Research: From the research findings, participants inputs from interviews, secondary research data were analyzed to identify similarities/patterns in user behavior and preference.A persona was built that resonates with their goals, needs and frustrations. A back-story for the persona was created to resemble interviewees lifestyle. Following which an empathy map was developed using patterns observed from inputs provided by participants from contextual inquiry. Two of the most observed pain points from the research data were chosen as underlying problem to create a scenario for storyboard. It was observed that the seller participants were hesitant to cancel the meetings and setting up a new meeting with a short notice. The story depicts a situation where the persona ‘Paula’ would face a similar problem and how using the designed calendar app would help her overcome her difficulties at work.
Paula Schneide
Field Executive

Paula is 27 years old. She has been with Contoso Corp for almost 3 years and mostly handles the sales by meeting monthly targets by visiting customers sites in India and pitching the solutions. She is a highly motivated individual. Last month she was awarded as “Rookie Salesman”.

Daniel Schmidt
Senior Sales Executive

Daniel is 35 years old. He is been working for Contoso corp for almost 15 years and he knows everything about Contoso’s sales cycles. Being a busy person, he keeps on travelling across the globe meeting with high value customers.

Heiko Peters
Customer

Heiko, is 30 years old. He is working for Fabricam Inc. as an Operation’s Manager and is responsible for handling the day to day operations of the company.

Storyboard & Design Solution

Before beginning to design wireframes, a story was outlined to explain the scenario. The story explain the overall situation and the solution. The basic structure of app was made with the use of sitemap. A low fidelity wireframe was designed using Adobe XD for important pages. Subsequently pages were later developed to complete task flow. Storyline is mentioned in Blue text next to the wireframes. The links in wireframes also demonstrates the overall flow.
