CityNext

Transforming cities to empower citizens

Role: Designer Team: Solution Strategy (Public Sector) | Architect | Digital Advisor Client: Department of Health (Australia) Device: Mobile & Tablet (Android App) Duration: 6 Month | 2018

Project Overview
Context

The city of Woden Valley, Australia has an operations center that focuses on citizens and how technology and innovation can support their everyday lives while seamlessly collecting data that can be monitored over time on devices and services platform.

Problem Statement

Citizena are facing an era, when it's getting more and more important to address enviromental concerns such as climate change, pollution or over population. By 2050, more than 60% of the world's population is expected to live in cities.

Solution

Our approach to the information systems and communication technologies we can incresae sustainbility. These solutions could be beneficial for both the citizens, the goverment and the enviroment.

CityNext apps, help to see the live enviromental data of your city, provided by sensors. Public can monitor the various enviromental attributes and get real time notifications about the possible dangers. The apps provides a direct connection between the public and the information system of the city.

How technology can this be helpful?

The machine learning and big data solutions could be one approach to improve and optimize the life in cities. A smart city usually is an urban area the uses various types of electronic data, collected by (mostly) sensors. The data will get structured in order for machine learning algorithm. This algorithm identifies patterns and learns from them. With that information, the goverment can manage assets and resources in an efficient and sustainable way to maximize the healthy growth of the city. It would be capable of predicting possible anomalies and enviromental changes. Beside that, the government could get a clear view of the life and development of the city.


Steps
1. Collecting- The various types of sensors collecting live data from all over the city, and sending them towards the cleaning process.
3. Learning- The prepared data is used by the algorithms, to learn the patterns and identify the possible anomalies.
2. Cleaning- The gathered raw data is getting cleaned and organized automatically, to give a foundation to the machine learning algorithms.
4. Predicting- With enough data and learning. the algorithms could predict possible, regularly occurring environmental changes.

Final Design

The goal of the application is to provide an accessible tool, between the public and the data channels. Beside that, the application can give the user, useful feedbacks and notification, in case of an unusaual enviromental change. They can customize the widgets and the prediction alogorithm in a way, to show them only the most essential and important notification which relevane to them.


The Dashboard (Visualising the data)

With the basic infos that the user provided during the set up process, the application compasses a widget structure, from the wide range of pre created widgets. The user will be able to change the arrangement and the widgets at anytime.

The widget system

In the widget library, the user can check all the information, coming from a specific location, that they set up earlier. There might be temporary widgets too, in case of an unusual enviromental anomaly, like an earthquake. By clicking on a widget, we can get a deeper look the the data and see all the attributes.

Air quality

Air quality could be one the most importan aspect, when you're going oitside for a walk, or choosing your next place to live. Living in an area with bad air quality or high traffic could shorten your life with years! With a live feed, you can always check, not just the overall pollution level, but also the ratio of each polllution, in case of you or one of your family member is sensible or allergic to that.

Enviromental danger

In some places it's not unusual that a rare enviromental danger occurs. It could be an earthquake, a tsunami, a tornado or even a forest fire. With the application, you can follow the live data and feedback, to make sure you're in the safest place. With enough information, the machine learning algorithm could predict these danger factors even before occurring. This function combined with a notification system could save lives.

Online tax filling

Simplifying financial lives for public. Online tax filling are designed to take the pain out of tedious income tax work. File IT tax returns Online effortlessly or invest into intelligent tax savings options in minutes with zero paperwork!

Quality of your area

Are you searching for a new home for you and your family? Wouldn't if be useful to see the quality of that area before moving there? With the application, you can check the live data, monitor it and make sure it's the right place for you and your family. By swiping the timelines, you can check the history of the area and the environmental attributes.

Look up different locations

With various types of sensors along the city, it would be possible to roam around the area from your smartphone the check the enviromental attributes of different locations. This function could be useful whenever you are thinking about moving to new place, going for a walk, or just visitng a new city part that you are not familiar with.
