Vision Demonstrators

Are ‘visions’ for our various customers

Role: Designer Responsiblities: Personas, Scenario Building, Interaction Design, Visual Design Team: Solution Architect | Salesteam | Digital Advisor Project: Microsoft Customer

Overview
Context

Vision demonstrators are ‘visions’ for various customers. A pre-sales activity, these are complete walk through of a conceptual application through scenarios of different personas interacting with the system. They touch upon different capabilities, features and technology stack.

I designed a number of these in the past 3 years touching upon various technology stacks from Hololens, Bots to mobile/tablet apps. These visions help to sell new projects to potential customers. Often, the real time projects are the outcome of these vision demonstrators.

Process

Personas and scenarios are identified. Thereafter information architecture is made, wireframes and then the visual design. These visions often go beyond constraints and involve minimal user research. They are done within a short time span of 2-3 weeks.

Example

Contact Center Modernization VD - Contoso Bank

The scenario – Post Covid scenario, the not so tech savvy customers, instead of going to bank have started calling the Call Centres to receive the required services. To a large set of customers, the call centres have become the face of the bank instead of the actual Tellers, front office executives etc. It therefore becomes extremely important for the banks to modernize their contact centres and provide the customers an enhanced experience where they feel more/better connected with the bank. The call centres should be enhanced to experience centres where the bank can give the similar experience to customers calling the call centre as if they are visiting the bank in person.
