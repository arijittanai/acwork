About Me

User Experience Designer with a passion for Accessibility and Inclusive Design


Hey there! Thank you for visiting here to get to know me better!

I studied Computer Science, but soon realized design is what I passionate about and I believe in inclusive and accessible experiences and products will benefit and impact everyone lifes, not only people with disabilities.

Spending my life with a joined family, where I live with my physically challenged cousin sister. I have seen her struggle with so many things in her daily life, but also I witnessed science and technology did help her and improve her quality of life. Thinking about UX and accessibility is naturally integrated into my life and now, I want to contribute to making the world a better place.

I am passionate about integrating the perspective of accessiblity and inclusive design into any project at the beginning, so products can reach out to more diverse customers and teams do not have to re-think a product life cycle after releasing. It's a win-win solution.

My personal interest is conversation design (learning about people's backgrounds, motivations, challenges. Everyone has an interesting story, it's just waiting to be discovered) and haptic feedback that I'm self-learning now :)

I'd love to hear from you! Feel free to contact me.
